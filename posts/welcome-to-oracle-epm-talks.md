# Welcome to Oracle EPM Talks

If you've ever spent hours debugging a FDMEE mapping rule, wondered why your Essbase outline is ballooning in size, or tried to explain Data Management to a business user — this blog is for you.

Oracle EPM Talks is a space for practitioners: consultants, admins, developers, and finance teams who work with Oracle's EPM suite every day.

## What We Cover

- **Oracle Planning & Budgeting (EPBCS / PBCS)** — forms, business rules, calc scripts, and best practices
- **Essbase** — outline design, performance tuning, MDX, and migrations
- **Enterprise Data Management (EDM)** — governance, pipelines, and integrations
- **FDMEE / Data Management** — mappings, load rules, troubleshooting
- **Narrative Reporting** — report design and tips
- **Cloud migrations** — moving from on-premise to OCI and EPM Cloud

## Who This Is For

Whether you're new to the Oracle EPM world or a seasoned architect, you'll find practical content here — not just theory.

Let's get into it.
